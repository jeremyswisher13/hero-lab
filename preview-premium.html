Featured Study

Magnesium L‑Threonate & the cognitive edge.

Pilot Results · AMSSM 2026 NCT07015047

Effects of Magnesium L-Threonate on sleep, recovery, and neurocognitive performance in collegiate athletes.

In a randomized, double-blind, placebo-controlled pilot trial of 107 NCAA Division I athletes at UCLA, four weeks of nightly Magnesium L-Threonate (1 g) yielded measurable advantages in cognitive reaction time and sleep quantity over placebo — effects that emerged within two to three weeks and held across both wearable and subjective measures.

p = 0.001
+54.6min
